# What is booth?

booth is a full-stack ticketing system app that I am building, targeted at teams and organisations with varying technical knowledge, to assist in translating non-technical requests into technical solutions.

The plan of the app is to create an internal system for task management and assignment within a team/organisation, and improve the simplicity of cross-functional collaboration. booth will also integrate with common technologies that all organisations are using, allowing for easy implementation of booth into their internal ecosystem.

The purpose of booth is not to replace the existing software within the space, such as Jira and Trello to name a couple. Instead, I want booth to sit on top, acting as the product owner, creating and assigning tickets and tasks, which can then be integrated and sit outside of booth on the team/organisation's preferred platforms and technologies.
